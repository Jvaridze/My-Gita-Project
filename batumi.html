Batumi

Batumi is the capital of the resort region of Georgia - Ajaria - its face and a trademark. Located in close proximity to the Turkish border, this fact is expressed in a variety of Turkish national cafes and restaurants scattered throughout the city, the smell of freshly brewed coffee on the sand and the Turkish language, which can be heard even in the winter. But Batumi is famous and attractive not only and not so much of it. Batumi is perfect by all - the picturesque seaside Boulevard with stunning palm trees along the coastline. By the sea with frolicking and gladdening the eyes of tourists dolphins; by narrow streets, where you can rest at a table of cozy cafes or simply walk; and, of course, by excellent taste of Adjarian cuisine - the eponymous khachapuri, borano, kaimagi, malahto... could not leave anyone indifferent.

infrastructure

Infrastructure in Batumi is very much developed. Hotels for every taste, local and interurban transportation, the cable car, the airport. On the beach: rental points of beach chairs and sun umbrellas, water attractions. On the boulevard you can rent a bicycle. For children there are all kinds of swings, carousel, Ferris wheel.Clothing, food and fish markets. At the last one for you will not only sell, but also prepare the chosen specimen of aquatic fauna of Georgia.

Attractions

Sure it is good to be on the sea, but you certainly would want to see the city and get to know it a bit more. Here is a chort overview of attractions. At «Piazza» Square you will temporarily travers to Western Europe. Cathedral of the Blessed Virgin. This cathedral in the Gothic style is relatively young in terms of the Georgian church architecture, but has already become one of the symbols of Batumi.

Argonauts Square with the statue of Medea will help to recall the myths and legends of ancient Greece. Also it is the venue of various celebrations and events...
